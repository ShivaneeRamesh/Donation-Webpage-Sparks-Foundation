About Us
We are a Non-governmental organization for welfare in the education field. We support the most vulnerable children to access their right to education through innovative ideas such as forming student groups and child-tracking systems, holding awareness workshops with parents to ensure that they send children to school. We also provides placement & apprenticeship opportunities to ITI, BTech, and diploma students for their bright future.
